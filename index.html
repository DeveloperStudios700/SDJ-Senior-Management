Rules & Information

1. Intellectual Property Theft

Any unauthorized copying, usage, redistribution, or modification of assets, scripts, systems, or intellectual property owned by SDJ Productions or affiliated entities is strictly prohibited and will result in enforcement action.

2. Asset Redistribution & Leakage

Distribution of proprietary assets across any platform, whether public or private, without explicit authorization is considered a severe violation.

3. False Ownership Claims

Claiming ownership over assets not originally created or licensed to you will result in immediate enforcement escalation.

4. Misrepresentation

Impersonation of SDJ staff, developers, or affiliated partners is strictly prohibited.

5. Harassment & Abuse

Any abusive, threatening, or disrespectful behavior toward staff or community members will not be tolerated.

6. Ban Evasion

Attempting to bypass enforcement actions via alternate accounts or indirect access methods will result in permanent restriction.

7. Evidence Tampering

Altering, deleting, or falsifying evidence during investigations will result in immediate escalation.

8. Repeated Violations

Users with prior offenses will face progressively stricter penalties based on historical records.

9. Compliance Enforcement Authority

All decisions made by SDJ Senior Management are final and are enforced based on internal review protocols.

10. System Integrity Protection

Any attempt to exploit, manipulate, or interfere with internal systems will result in immediate enforcement action.

Notice: Enforcement actions may include temporary suspension, permanent blacklisting, or extended restrictions depending on severity, supporting evidence, and prior conduct history.
